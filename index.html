About Me

I am a hardworking and dependable research professional experienced in both qualitative and clinical research. Experience with in-depth qualitative interviews, survey research, and questionnaire research methodology. Coordinated HIV and other STI vaccine studies, as well as various treatment trials for both NIH-funded grants and pharmaceutical companies. Conducted data collection, data analysis, and project management with a particular research interest in HIV/STI prevention, health disparities, and long-term care reform for high risk populations. Presented on Fathers with Children in Multiple Household Study in Sydney Australia, Co-authored on several journal articles with my research team that are in the process of being published for year 2016. Also, a Certified HIV Education Counselor with the State of Illinois.
